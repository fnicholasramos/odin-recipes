Coffee Jelly

Coffee jelly is a delightful dessert with soft, jiggly cubes of rich and coffee-flavored.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of brewed coffee (strong)
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon of gelatin powder
  • 1/4 cup of water (for dissolving gelatin)
  • Whipped cream or sweetened condensed milk (for topping, optional)

Steps:

  1. Brew Coffee: Brew 2 cups of strong coffee and mix in 2 tablespoons of sugar while it's hot. Stir until the sugar dissolves, then set aside.
  2. Dissolve Gelatin: In a small bowl, dissolve 1 tablespoon of gelatin powder in 1/4 cup of warm water. Stir well and let it sit for a minute or two.
  3. Combine: Pour the dissolved gelatin mixture into the hot coffee and stir until fully combined.
  4. Chill: Pour the mixture into a shallow dish or container and let it cool to room temperature. Once cooled, refrigerate for 2-4 hours or until fully set.
  5. Cut into Cubes: Once the jelly is firm, cut it into small cubes.
  6. Serve: Spoon the coffee jelly into glasses or bowls. Top with whipped cream or drizzle with sweetened condensed milk for extra indulgence.
